Healthcare reporting & workflow automation
Siddhartha Institute of Medical Sciences  ·  India  ·  Sep 2019–Jun 2022
What I observed
Manual enrollment reporting, inconsistent data across systems, and no formal access control — a significant risk in a healthcare institution handling sensitive records.
What I resolved
Introduced structured data governance from the ground up: role-based access, validation checks, and controlled distribution. Coordinated with IT for compliance-ready deployment.
What I built
Power BI dashboards with role-specific DAX views, Power Automate flows for reporting automation, and Azure SQL relational models as the structured data layer.
What made this different
This was my first exposure to building systems in a sensitive data environment — and it shaped how I approach every project since. I introduced access controls and audit trails as a design-first principle, not an afterthought. That practice is directly relevant to working in an anesthesiology practice where PHI handling is non-negotiable.
